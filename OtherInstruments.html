Other Instruments

Other instruments Arjun plays are:

Organ (teacher - Dr. Ray Landers)

Harp (teacher - Dr. John Sheridan)

Veena (teachers - Dr. Gopal Kumar; Smt. Hemalatha Veeramani)

Persian Santur, Tar, and Tonbak (teacher - Maestro Ahmad Borhani)

Violin (teacher - Catherine Boyd)

His harp performance won him a competition award from Music Fest (2010)

His veena performance won him competition awards from Carnatic Music Association of North America (2008, 2010), Thyagaraja Aradhana Festival, Cleveland, Ohio (2011, 2012).

He has played Santur publicly several times with Persian National Music Ensemble.
